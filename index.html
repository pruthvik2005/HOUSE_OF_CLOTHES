Our Legacy

Weaving Stories Since Generations

House of Clothes is born out of love for Indian handloom and traditional craftsmanship. Every weave tells a story of dedicated artisans who spend weeks blending silk threads, gorgeous motifs, and patterns into classy garments.

Our kurtis are designed for the modern woman who cherishes her roots, featuring hand-embroidery like Chikankari and Block prints on high-quality fabrics that are comfortable and elegant.
